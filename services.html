Wedding Entertainment

Birthday Parties

From £200 / set

30th, 40th, 50th — whatever age, Dion will arrive, decline your tap water, eat something off the buffet without asking, and within ten minutes have everyone convinced he's been their mate for years. Hen & Stag Dos

From £150 / set

Dion thrives in chaos. The louder the room, the better he gets. He'll arrive appearing sober — an illusion that will either hold all evening or collapse spectacularly around the 40-minute mark, at which point he will begin doing impressions and pointing at strangers. Inexplicably, everyone loves it. He is the most sober-adjacent person in the room. That's the best we can offer.

  • 30 minute high-energy set
  • Games & audience participation
  • Will flirt with at least one person. He can't help it.
